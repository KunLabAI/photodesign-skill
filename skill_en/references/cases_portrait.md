# Portrait Cases

## POR-001 Dreamy Wide-Aperture Headshot

- Applicability: soft-light headshots, romantic portraits, emotional close-ups, single character face design
- Equipment: Canon EOS R5 + Canon RF 85mm f/1.2L USM
- Settings: f/1.2-f/1.4 | ISO 100 | 3200K warm | 45° loop lighting | window light or golden-hour backlight
- Image result: razor-sharp eyes, rim-lit hair, large creamy background bokeh
- Prompt template: Shot on Canon EOS R5 with RF 85mm f/1.2L at f/1.2, 3200K warm color temperature, 45° loop lighting, close-up portrait of [subject], one eye in critical focus with visible eyelash detail, warm natural rim light on hair, extremely shallow depth of field, background dissolved into large creamy bokeh orbs, flattering Canon skin tones, intimate dreamy atmosphere.
- Replace: [subject], hair color, expression, bokeh color, time of day, color temperature
- Avoid: group shots, full-body fashion detail, backgrounds that must remain readable
