# Props and Products Cases

## PRD-001 Mechanical Prop Macro

- Applicability: mechanical arms, gears, steampunk objects, leather and metal props
- Equipment: Hasselblad X2D 100C + XCD 120mm Macro
- Settings: f/5.6 | ISO 64 | studio macro light plus rim light
- Image result: screws, scratches, leather grain, and metal edges are cleanly separated
- Prompt template: Shot on Hasselblad X2D 100C with XCD 120mm Macro at f/5.6, extreme close-up product photograph of [prop], every rivet, gear, scratch, and leather grain rendered in medium format detail, dark moody studio background, precise rim light accentuating metal edges, shallow layered depth.
- Replace: [prop], metal type, leather color, background material
- Avoid: f/1.2 depth that hides structure, plastic-looking metal
