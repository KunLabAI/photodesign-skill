# Full-Body Character Cases

## CHR-001 Sharp Modern Full Body

- Applicability: game characters, modern warriors, cyberpunk, complete outfit and equipment display
- Equipment: Sony A7 IV + Sigma 35mm f/1.4 DG DN Art
- Settings: f/2.8 | ISO 200 | 4000K/7000K mixed neon | 135° side-back rim light | mixed neon and ambient light
- Image result: head-to-boots clarity, readable environment, sharp gear edges
- Prompt template: Captured with Sony A7 IV and Sigma 35mm f/1.4 Art at f/2.8, mixed 4000K/7000K neon color temperature, 135° side-back rim lighting, full-body character design of [subject] standing in [environment], sharp from head to boots with controlled depth of field, razor-sharp clothing and gear details, background neon glow and wet surface reflections, modern high-resolution concept photography.
- Replace: [subject], outfit, gear, environment, neon colors, color temperature
- Avoid: 85mm ultra-shallow depth that blurs feet, unreadable backgrounds

## CHR-002 High-Fashion Full Body

- Applicability: fashion editorial, runway styling, commercial full-body hero shot, textile detail
- Equipment: Canon EOS R5 + RF 50mm f/1.2L
- Settings: f/2-f/2.8 | ISO 100 | 5500K studio | 15° butterfly lighting | studio flash plus colored gels
- Image result: face and clothing remain sharp, clean background, refined fabric texture
- Prompt template: Shot on Canon EOS R5 with RF 50mm f/1.2L at f/2.2, 5500K studio color temperature, 15° butterfly lighting, full-body high-fashion photograph of [subject] wearing [wardrobe], clean studio cyclorama, sculpted softbox lighting with subtle colored gel accents, flattering skin tone, crisp textile texture, editorial magazine composition.
- Replace: [subject], wardrobe, gel color, background color, pose, lighting angle
- Avoid: cluttered street scenes, too-shallow depth, low-detail fabric

## CHR-003 Fantasy Warrior

- Applicability: armor, robes, weapons, fantasy character design
- Equipment: Nikon Z8 + Nikkor Z 50mm f/1.2 S
- Settings: f/2.5-f/4 | ISO 400 | 5000K daylight | 90° side lighting | side light plus mist fill
- Image result: readable armor texture and pose, layered foggy background
- Prompt template: Shot on Nikon Z8 with Nikkor Z 50mm f/1.2 S at f/2.8, 5000K daylight color temperature, 90° side lighting, full-body portrait of [subject] in ornate fantasy armor, standing in [environment], accurate color reproduction on metal scratches and patina, medium depth of field separating the character from mist, dramatic side lighting and heroic silhouette.
- Replace: [subject], armor material, weapon, battlefield/temple/forest, lighting angle
- Avoid: strong wide-angle distortion, half-body crops, fog hiding armor detail

## CHR-004 Adventuring Party Group

- Applicability: four-person party, ensemble cast, tabletop or game promotional art
- Equipment: Sony A7R V + FE 35mm f/1.4 GM
- Settings: f/4-f/5.6 | ISO 200 | 4000K warm daylight | 45° side lighting | golden-hour side light
- Image result: all faces sharp, natural spacing, no facial distortion
- Prompt template: Captured with Sony A7R V and FE 35mm f/1.4 GM at f/4.5, 4000K warm daylight color temperature, 45° side lighting, group portrait of [number] [subjects] standing in formation before [environment], sufficient depth of field keeping every face and costume sharp, natural 35mm perspective, golden side light casting long shadows, cohesive ensemble design.
- Replace: number, class mix, environment, formation, time of day, color temperature
- Avoid: f/1.2 depth, ultra-wide close-up distortion, characters blocking each other

## CHR-005 Environmental Narrative Character

- Applicability: character in home/workplace, narrative concept art, profession design
- Equipment: Leica Q3 + Summilux 28mm f/1.7
- Settings: f/4-f/5.6 | ISO 400 | 5000K daylight | 60° natural side light | available practical light
- Image result: full character and environment both readable, documentary atmosphere
- Prompt template: Shot on Leica Q3 with Summilux 28mm f/1.7 at f/4, 5000K daylight color temperature, 60° natural side light, environmental full-body portrait of [subject] inside [space], the character framed by meaningful objects and lived-in details, natural perspective with mild wide-angle depth, available light, rich Leica mid-tones, documentary design realism.
- Replace: [subject], space, professional props, walls/windows/desk details, color temperature
- Avoid: pure studio backgrounds, character-only framing, extreme perspective distortion

## CHR-006 Sci-Fi Tactical Character

- Applicability: mecha suits, powered armor, tactical clothing, cold sci-fi design
- Equipment: RED V-RAPTOR + Sigma Cine 40mm T1.5
- Settings: T2.8 | ISO 800 | 4000K/7000K mixed sci-fi | 90° overhead plus side | cool overhead light plus screen glow
- Image result: crisp technical detail across metal, glass, and fabric
- Prompt template: Filmed on RED V-RAPTOR with Sigma Cine 40mm T1.5 at T2.8, mixed 4000K/7000K sci-fi color temperature, 90° overhead plus side lighting, full-body sci-fi tactical character [subject] in [environment], crisp high-resolution armor panels, technical fabric and visor reflections, cool overhead light mixed with screen glow, controlled depth of field, sleek futuristic production still.
- Replace: [subject], armor type, base/ship/lab, screen color, color temperature
- Avoid: warm vintage film, loose silhouettes, excessive haze

## CHR-007 Classical Costume Portrait

- Applicability: historical costume, court dress, traditional garments, full-body classical portrait
- Equipment: Fujifilm GFX 100S + GF 80mm f/1.7
- Settings: f/4 | ISO 100 | 4000K warm daylight | 45° window light | large window light plus reflector
- Image result: fine fabric, embroidery, and skin tone with painterly background
- Prompt template: Photographed with Fujifilm GFX 100S and GF 80mm f/1.7 at f/4, 4000K warm daylight color temperature, 45° window light, full-length classical portrait of [subject] wearing [costume], large window light shaping the face and fabric folds, medium format tonal gradation, delicate embroidery and textile texture, painterly yet photographic atmosphere.
- Replace: [subject], costume culture, interior background, fabric, pose, color temperature
- Avoid: close wide-angle, modern street elements, blurred garment edges

## CHR-008 Action Freeze

- Applicability: jumping, running, swordplay, dance, rain action
- Equipment: Canon EOS R3 + RF 70-200mm f/2.8L IS
- Settings: 100mm f/2.8 | ISO 800 | 5500K daylight | 135° side-back rim lighting | high-speed sync flash or strong side light
- Image result: frozen action, compressed background, readable droplets and fabric motion
- Prompt template: Captured with Canon EOS R3 and RF 70-200mm f/2.8L at 100mm f/2.8, 5500K daylight color temperature, 135° side-back rim lighting, full-body action freeze of [subject] performing [action], fabric and droplets suspended in midair, compressed background blur, dramatic side light, sharp dynamic pose with professional sports-photography timing.
- Replace: [subject], action, weather, fabric material, background, lighting angle
- Avoid: slow-shutter smear, low shutter blur, static standing poses
