# Scene Environment Cases

## ENV-001 Cyberpunk City

- Applicability: night cities, megastructures, neon streets, environment concept art
- Equipment: Nikon Z8 + Nikkor Z 14-24mm f/2.8 S
- Settings: 16mm f/8 | ISO 400 | high-dynamic-range urban night
- Image result: foreground streets and distant towers remain clear with strong perspective lines
- Prompt template: Captured with Nikon Z8 and Nikkor Z 14-24mm f/2.8 S at 16mm f/8, wide environmental photograph of [city/district], deep depth of field from foreground street details to distant megastructures, dramatic converging perspective lines, neon signs, steam, wet pavement reflections, clean high-ISO shadows and cinematic urban scale.
- Replace: [city/district], neon colors, weather, foreground elements, distant architecture
- Avoid: portrait-style shallow depth, telephoto compression that closes the space
