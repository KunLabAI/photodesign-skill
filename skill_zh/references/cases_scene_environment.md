# 场景环境案例

## ENV-001 赛博朋克城市

- 适用：城市夜景、巨型建筑、霓虹街区、环境概念图
- 器材：Nikon Z8 + Nikkor Z 14-24mm f/2.8 S
- 参数：16mm f/8 | ISO 400 | 城市夜景高动态范围
- 画面结果：前景街道到远处高楼都清晰，透视线强，暗部干净
- 提示词模板：Captured with Nikon Z8 and Nikkor Z 14-24mm f/2.8 S at 16mm f/8, wide environmental photograph of [城市/街区], deep depth of field from foreground street details to distant megastructures, dramatic converging perspective lines, neon signs, steam, wet pavement reflections, clean high-ISO shadows and cinematic urban scale.
- 可替换变量：[城市/街区]、霓虹颜色、天气、前景元素、远景建筑
- 避免：浅景深人像写法、长焦压缩导致空间打不开

## ENV-002 奇幻自然景观

- 适用：森林、山谷、瀑布、魔法生态、游戏场景原画
- 器材：Sony A7R V + FE 16-35mm f/2.8 GM II
- 参数：20mm f/11 | ISO 100 | 黄金时刻或薄雾散射光
- 画面结果：前景植物和远景山体都有层次，景深大，色彩清爽
- 提示词模板：Shot on Sony A7R V with FE 16-35mm f/2.8 GM II at 20mm f/11, expansive fantasy landscape of [自然场景], deep depth of field from foreground plants and stones to distant waterfalls and cliffs, golden light filtering through atmospheric mist, ultra-wide field of view, crisp ecological detail and luminous color.
- 可替换变量：[自然场景]、植物类型、魔法元素、天气、时间段
- 避免：背景完全虚化、人物占比过大、过度黑暗

## ENV-003 室内氛围

- 适用：图书馆、茶室、咖啡馆、工作室、角色居住空间
- 器材：Fujifilm GFX 100S + GF 32-64mm f/4 R LM WR
- 参数：45mm f/4 | ISO 800 | 实景灯 + 窗光
- 画面结果：中画幅暗部层次丰富，前中后景都有可读信息
- 提示词模板：Shot on Fujifilm GFX 100S with GF 32-64mm at 45mm f/4, atmospheric interior photograph of [室内空间], warm practical lights mixed with soft window light, medium format shadow detail and tonal gradation, foreground furniture, mid-ground objects, and background architecture all readable, quiet lived-in mood.
- 可替换变量：[室内空间]、时代、灯具、家具材质、窗外天气
- 避免：过度广角让家具变形、没有层次的平光

## ENV-004 荒野废墟

- 适用：末日公路、废弃城市、荒野设施、孤独环境
- 器材：Leica SL2 + APO-Summicron-SL 28mm f/2
- 参数：f/5.6 | ISO 200 | 阴天散射光
- 画面结果：衰败细节柔和清晰，色彩克制，气氛忧郁
- 提示词模板：Captured with Leica SL2 and APO-Summicron-SL 28mm f/2 at f/5.6, post-apocalyptic environment of [废墟场景], abandoned structures overgrown with vegetation, wide angle encompassing the desolate landscape, overcast diffused light, melancholic Leica color rendering, detailed textures of rust, concrete, and weeds.
- 可替换变量：[废墟场景]、植被、天气、遗留物、人物是否出现
- 避免：强烈商业光、过鲜艳色彩、干净无破损的空间

## ENV-005 建筑内景

- 适用：教堂、博物馆、未来大厅、宏大室内空间
- 器材：Canon EOS R5 + RF 15-35mm f/2.8L IS
- 参数：15-18mm f/8 | ISO 200 | 自然天窗光或室内均衡光
- 画面结果：垂直线尽量规整，空间纵深和尺度强烈
- 提示词模板：Shot on Canon EOS R5 with RF 15-35mm f/2.8L at 16mm f/8, architectural interior photograph of [建筑空间], towering vertical lines, symmetrical composition, deep depth of field, natural skylight revealing material surfaces, clean wide-angle perspective, grand spatial scale without excessive distortion.
- 可替换变量：[建筑空间]、材质、风格、光源、是否有人物作比例
- 避免：近距离人物脸部、极端鱼眼畸变、浅景深

## ENV-006 海岸氛围

- 适用：海边孤独人物、雾气、清晨冷暖交界、情绪场景
- 器材：Leica Q3 + Summilux 28mm f/1.7
- 参数：f/5.6 | ISO 200 | 清晨薄雾自然光
- 画面结果：人物或物体与海岸环境关系清楚，色彩柔和
- 提示词模板：Shot on Leica Q3 with Summilux 28mm at f/5.6, atmospheric coastal scene of [主体/场景] near the shoreline, soft morning mist, muted blues and warm highlights, wet sand reflections, natural wide perspective, quiet human-scale composition with refined Leica color transitions.
- 可替换变量：[主体/场景]、海岸类型、天气、潮水、道具
- 避免：强烈饱和度、旅游宣传照式艳阳天、主体太近变形

## ENV-007 雪地冷调

- 适用：雪原、极地基地、冷调孤独场景、极简构图
- 器材：Sony A7R V + FE 24-70mm f/2.8 GM II
- 参数：35mm f/8 | ISO 100 | 阴天雪面反光
- 画面结果：白色层次不死白，冷调细腻，主体轮廓清楚
- 提示词模板：Captured with Sony A7R V and FE 24-70mm f/2.8 GM II at 35mm f/8, minimalist snowfield scene of [主体/建筑], subtle texture in white snow, cold blue-gray atmosphere, overcast sky acting as a giant softbox, deep depth of field, crisp silhouette and restrained high-resolution detail.
- 可替换变量：[主体/建筑]、雪面痕迹、天空、远景山体
- 避免：高光全过曝、过暖色调、背景杂乱

## ENV-008 沙漠史诗

- 适用：沙漠远征、巨型遗迹、热浪、史诗远景
- 器材：Sony A1 + FE 100-400mm f/4.5-5.6 GM OSS
- 参数：300mm f/8 | ISO 200 | 低角度夕阳
- 画面结果：远景被压缩，太阳、人物、遗迹形成巨大比例关系
- 提示词模板：Shot on Sony A1 with FE 100-400mm GM at 300mm f/8, epic desert scene of [主体] crossing dunes before [远景], strong telephoto compression stacking dunes, ruins, and sun into layered planes, heat haze in the distance, low golden light, dramatic scale and restrained color palette.
- 可替换变量：[主体]、远景、沙丘形态、太阳位置、队伍规模
- 避免：超广角导致史诗压缩消失、近景凌乱

## ENV-009 城市街头

- 适用：夜市、雨夜街道、纪实城市氛围、烟火气
- 器材：Fujifilm X-T5 + XF 23mm f/1.4 R LM WR
- 参数：f/2.8 | ISO 800 | 商铺灯和街灯
- 画面结果：人群、招牌、蒸汽和街道细节自然可读
- 提示词模板：Shot on Fujifilm X-T5 with XF 23mm f/1.4 at f/2.8, documentary street scene of [街区/夜市], shop lights and street lamps mixing warm and cool tones, readable crowd gestures, steam, signs, and wet pavement, natural perspective, lively but grounded urban atmosphere.
- 可替换变量：[街区/夜市]、摊位、天气、人群密度、色彩倾向
- 避免：完全无人、过度科幻霓虹、棚拍式干净

## ENV-010 奢华室内

- 适用：酒店大堂、商业空间、精品店、豪宅室内
- 器材：Hasselblad X2D 100C + XCD 38mm f/2.5
- 参数：f/8 | ISO 64 | 室内均衡灯光
- 画面结果：材质、线条、空间比例高级，色彩准确克制
- 提示词模板：Photographed with Hasselblad X2D 100C and XCD 38mm f/2.5 at f/8, luxury interior photograph of [空间], polished stone, wood, glass, and metal materials rendered with medium format clarity, balanced architectural lighting, clean lines, accurate color, premium hospitality design atmosphere.
- 可替换变量：[空间]、材质、品牌调性、家具、灯光色温
- 避免：过度广角变形、廉价过饱和、人物喧宾夺主
