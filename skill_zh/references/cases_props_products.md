# 道具与产品案例

## PRD-001 机械道具微距

- 适用：机械臂、齿轮、蒸汽朋克、皮革和金属混合道具
- 器材：Hasselblad X2D 100C + XCD 120mm Macro
- 参数：f/5.6 | ISO 64 | 色温 5500K | 光照角度 120°侧逆光 | 影棚微距灯 + 轮廓光
- 画面结果：螺丝、划痕、皮革纹理清晰，背景深暗分离
- 提示词模板：Shot on Hasselblad X2D 100C with XCD 120mm Macro at f/5.6, 5500K studio color temperature, 120° rim lighting, extreme close-up product photograph of [道具], every rivet, gear, scratch, and leather grain rendered in medium format detail, dark moody studio background, precise rim light accentuating metal edges, shallow layered depth.
- 可替换变量：[道具]、金属类型、皮革颜色、背景材质、光照角度
- 避免：过浅 f/1.2 导致关键结构虚化、塑料质感金属

## PRD-002 珠宝精密布光

- 适用：戒指、项链、宝石、黄金、奢侈品广告
- 器材：Phase One IQ4 150MP + Schneider Kreuznach 120mm LS Macro
- 参数：f/8-f/11 | ISO 50 | 色温 5500K | 光照角度 90°侧光 | 多点控光 + 黑卡
- 画面结果：宝石切面、金属边缘和高光都可控，颜色准确
- 提示词模板：Photographed with Phase One IQ4 150MP and Schneider 120mm LS Macro at f/8, 5500K studio standard color temperature, 90° side lighting, luxury jewelry photograph of [道具] on [承托面], microscopic gemstone facets and gold filigree rendered with absolute clarity, controlled specular highlights, black flags shaping reflections, premium catalog-level color accuracy.
- 可替换变量：[道具]、宝石、金属、承托面、背景颜色、色温
- 避免：柔焦、杂乱反射、宝石颜色漂移

## PRD-003 武器细节

- 适用：刀剑、枪械、法杖、弓箭、武器局部纹理
- 器材：Nikon Z8 + Nikkor Z MC 105mm f/2.8 VR S
- 参数：f/5.6-f/8 | ISO 100 | 色温 5500K | 光照角度 90°侧光 | 侧光 + 反射板
- 画面结果：刃纹、木纹、皮革缠绕和铭文清晰
- 提示词模板：Shot on Nikon Z8 with Z MC 105mm Macro at f/5.6, 5500K studio color temperature, 90° side lighting, detailed product photograph of [武器], macro focus revealing [关键纹理], diffused side lighting creating controlled gradients along the blade or surface, charcoal background falloff, crisp micro-contrast and realistic material response.
- 可替换变量：[武器]、关键纹理、摆放方式、背景材质、光照角度
- 避免：动作场景抢走主体、反光过曝、武器比例变形

## PRD-004 透明容器与液体

- 适用：药水瓶、香氛瓶、酒杯、实验容器、发光液体
- 器材：Canon EOS R5 + RF 100mm f/2.8L Macro IS
- 参数：f/3.5-f/5.6 | ISO 200 | 色温 4000K | 光照角度 180°逆光 | 逆光 + 侧补光
- 画面结果：玻璃边缘、气泡、液体折射和背光光晕清楚
- 提示词模板：Shot on Canon EOS R5 with RF 100mm f/2.8L Macro at f/4, 4000K warm color temperature, 180° backlighting, close-up product photograph of [透明容器] filled with [液体], backlit to reveal bubbles, refraction, and glowing edges, subtle fill light on the label and cap, shallow background separation from [环境], premium magical realism.
- 可替换变量：[透明容器]、液体颜色、标签、背景环境、色温
- 避免：玻璃边缘消失、标签全虚、强反射遮挡主体

## PRD-005 奢侈品瓶身

- 提示词模板：Photographed with Hasselblad X2D 100C and XCD 90mm f/2.5 at f/8, 5500K studio standard color temperature, 90° side lighting, luxury product photograph of [瓶身产品] on reflective black acrylic, clean vertical highlight strips defining the glass edges, controlled metallic cap reflections, deep premium background, medium format color depth and immaculate advertising finish.
- 可替换变量：[瓶身产品]、液体颜色、瓶盖材质、背景色、色温
- 避免：生活化桌面、杂乱反射、过度广角导致瓶身变形

## PRD-006 腕表细节

- 避免：时间刻度模糊、玻璃反光遮挡、过重景深虚化
