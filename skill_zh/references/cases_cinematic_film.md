# 电影与胶片质感案例

## CIN-001 ARRI 叙事肖像

- 适用：电影静帧、角色内心戏、自然肤色、柔和高光
- 器材：ARRI ALEXA 35 + ARRI Signature Prime 75mm T1.8
- 参数：T2.0 | 800 ASA | 色温 3200K | 光照角度 45°伦勃朗光 | 实景灯 + 柔和负补光
- 画面结果：肤色自然，高光不过曝，阴影层次柔顺
- 提示词模板：Filmed on ARRI ALEXA 35 with Signature Prime 75mm at T2.0, 3200K warm practical color temperature, 45° Rembrandt lighting, cinematic close-up of [主体] in [场景], natural skin tones, soft highlight rolloff, gentle shadow detail, practical light motivating the scene, shallow depth of field, quiet narrative tension like a premium film still.
- 可替换变量：[主体]、场景、情绪、实景光源、色温、光照角度
- 避免：过度锐化数码感、无动机光源、夸张美颜

## CIN-002 Cooke 暖调内景

- 适用：暖色室内、复古酒吧、办公室、家庭夜景
- 器材：ARRI ALEXA Mini LF + Cooke S7/i 50mm T2.0
- 参数：T2.0-T2.8 | 800 ASA | 色温 3200K | 光照角度 60°侧光 | 台灯/壁灯等实景光
- 画面结果：Cooke Look 柔和亲肤，高光有轻微晕染，氛围温暖
- 提示词模板：Filmed on ARRI ALEXA Mini LF with Cooke S7/i 50mm T2.0, 3200K warm practical color temperature, 60° side lighting, warm cinematic interior of [主体/场景], practical lamps creating amber pools of light, gentle Cooke rendering on skin and highlights, smooth tonal transitions in shadows, shallow but readable background, intimate narrative mood.
- 可替换变量：[主体/场景]、灯具、年代、空间、情绪、色温
- 避免：冷硬科技光、超锐商业摄影、纯黑无细节

## CIN-003 RED 科幻冷调

- 适用：科幻基地、机甲、实验室、冷调高解析画面
- 器材：RED V-RAPTOR 8K VV + Sigma Cine 35mm T1.5
- 参数：T2.8 | ISO 800 | 色温 6500K | 光照角度 90°顶光 | 冷色顶光 + 屏幕光
- 画面结果：细节锐利，金属和玻璃边缘干净，科技感强
- 提示词模板：Captured on RED V-RAPTOR 8K VV with Sigma Cine 35mm at T2.8, 6500K cool color temperature, 90° overhead lighting, high-resolution sci-fi scene of [主体] inside [环境], cool overhead light and cyan screen glow, crisp metallic surfaces, glass reflections, clean shadows, precise futuristic production design with controlled depth of field.
- 可替换变量：[主体]、环境、屏幕颜色、科技材质、色温
- 避免：胶片粗颗粒、暖调怀旧、低清模糊

## CIN-004 独立电影夜景

- 适用：低预算电影感、街头夜戏、自然灯源、真实质感
- 器材：Blackmagic URSA Mini Pro 12K + Zeiss CP.3 35mm T2.1
- 参数：T2.8 | ISO 800-1250 | 色温 3200K/5500K混合 | 光照角度 60°侧光 | 路灯/便利店/车灯
- 画面结果：夜色有层次，噪点适度，人物融入真实街景
- 提示词模板：Shot on Blackmagic URSA Mini Pro 12K with Zeiss CP.3 35mm T2.1, mixed 3200K/5500K street color temperature, 60° side lighting, indie film night scene of [主体] in [街景], motivated light from storefronts, street lamps, and passing cars, natural contrast, subtle digital grain, grounded handheld stillness, realistic urban color.
- 可替换变量：[主体]、街景、光源、天气、动作、色温
- 避免：过度干净广告光、无噪点塑料夜景、超现实霓虹堆砌

## CIN-005 16mm 纪实

- 适用：纪录片、童年记忆、旅行、真实粗颗粒
- 器材：Arriflex 16SR + Zeiss Super Speed 25mm T1.3
- 参数：T2.8 | 色温 3200K | 光照角度 60°自然光 | Kodak Vision3 500T
- 画面结果：16mm 颗粒明显，动态范围柔和，画面有生活痕迹
- 提示词模板：Shot on Arriflex 16SR with Zeiss Super Speed 25mm on Kodak Vision3 500T, 3200K warm tungsten color temperature, 60° natural available light, 16mm documentary frame of [主体/场景], visible film grain, soft halation around practical lights, handheld candid composition, imperfect but emotional texture, warm memory-like realism.
- 可替换变量：[主体/场景]、地点、年代、动作、光源、色温
- 避免：超高商业锐度、过度干净皮肤、CG 感构图

## CIN-006 35mm 胶片剧照

- 适用：经典电影海报、复古剧照、成熟胶片色彩
- 器材：Panavision Panaflex Millennium XL2 + Panavision Primo 50mm T1.9
- 参数：T2.8 | 色温 3200K | 光照角度 45°侧光 | Kodak Vision3 250D 或 500T
- 画面结果：胶片高光柔顺，色彩厚实，剧照感强
- 提示词模板：Filmed on Panavision Panaflex Millennium XL2 with Primo 50mm at T2.8 on Kodak Vision3 film, 3200K warm cinematic color temperature, 45° side lighting, cinematic still of [主体] in [场景], rich film color, soft highlight rolloff, subtle grain, composed production design, balanced depth of field, classic theatrical movie atmosphere.
- 可替换变量：[主体]、场景、胶片类型、年代、服装、色温
- 避免：现代手机感、极端 HDR、廉价滤镜颗粒

## CIN-007 即时胶片记忆

- 适用：宝丽来、派对、亲密记忆、非完美曝光
- 器材：Polaroid SX-70
- 参数：即时胶片 | 色温 4000K | 光照角度 0°闪光 | 自然光或闪光灯
- 画面结果：边缘柔和、色彩偏暖、曝光略不完美
- 提示词模板：Shot on Polaroid SX-70 instant film, 4000K warm color temperature, 0° flash lighting, intimate snapshot of [主体/场景], soft edges, warm faded color, imperfect exposure, slight chemical texture, spontaneous framing, nostalgic personal memory feeling.
- 可替换变量：[主体/场景]、场合、闪光/自然光、年代、色温
- 避免：过度高清商业质感、精确棚拍布光、复杂远景

## CIN-008 低保真录像感

- 适用：VHS、90 年代录像、监控、低保真恐怖或怀旧
- 器材：Sony Handycam CCD-TRV 系列
- 参数：模拟录像 | 色温 4000K | 光照角度 0°自动曝光 | 室内弱光
- 画面结果：低分辨率、轻微色偏、扫描噪声和时间感
- 提示词模板：Captured on a 1990s Sony Handycam analog camcorder, 4000K warm indoor color temperature, 0° auto-exposure lighting, low-fidelity video still of [主体/场景], soft resolution, slight color bleed, scan noise, auto-exposure glow from practical lights, handheld framing, unsettling nostalgic VHS atmosphere.
- 可替换变量：[主体/场景]、年代、室内/室外、画面故障程度、色温
- 避免：干净 8K 质感、精致电影布光、无噪点高清
