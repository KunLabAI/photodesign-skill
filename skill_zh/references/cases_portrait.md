# 角色人像案例

## POR-001 梦幻大光圈头像

- 适用：柔光头像、浪漫写真、情绪特写、单人角色脸部设计
- 器材：Canon EOS R5 + Canon RF 85mm f/1.2L USM
- 参数：f/1.2-f/1.4 | ISO 100 | 色温 3200K | 光照角度 45°环形光 | 窗边自然光或黄金时刻逆光
- 画面结果：眼睛极锐，发丝有轮廓光，背景融化成大面积奶油焦外
- 提示词模板：Shot on Canon EOS R5 with RF 85mm f/1.2L at f/1.2, 3200K warm color temperature, 45° loop lighting, close-up portrait of [主体], one eye in critical focus with visible eyelash detail, warm natural rim light on hair, extremely shallow depth of field, background dissolved into large creamy bokeh orbs, flattering Canon skin tones, intimate dreamy atmosphere.
- 可替换变量：[主体]、发色、表情、背景光斑颜色、时间段、色温
- 避免：多人合影、全身服装展示、需要背景清晰交代的画面

## POR-002 锐利商业肖像

- 适用：商业头像、演员定妆、企业高端肖像、清晰皮肤和五官
- 器材：Sony A7R V + Sony FE 85mm f/1.4 GM II
- 参数：f/1.8-f/2.2 | ISO 100-200 | 色温 5500K | 光照角度 45°环形光 | 大柔光箱 + 弱补光
- 画面结果：面部从眼睛到耳朵清晰，肤质真实但不粗糙，背景干净
- 提示词模板：Captured with Sony A7R V and FE 85mm f/1.4 GM II at f/1.8, 5500K studio color temperature, 45° loop lighting, high-resolution studio portrait of [主体], tack-sharp facial features, controlled three-point lighting with a soft key light and subtle fill, clean neutral background, smooth GM bokeh transition, realistic skin texture rendered with premium commercial clarity.
- 可替换变量：[主体]、服装、背景色、灯光方向、商业品类、色温
- 避免：过度梦幻、强烈镜头眩光、复杂背景

## POR-003 中画幅质感肖像

- 参数：f/2.5-f/4 | ISO 64 | 色温 5500K | 光照角度 45°环形光 | 柔光箱或北向窗光

- 提示词模板：Photographed with Hasselblad X2D 100C and XCD 90mm f/2.5, 5500K studio color temperature, 45° loop lighting, medium format portrait of [主体], extraordinary tonal depth and subtle skin gradations, micro-contrast revealing texture without harshness, calm luxury mood, clean studio space, soft falloff from highlight to shadow.
- 可替换变量：[主体]、品牌气质、服装材质、背景明度、色温
- 避免：夸张色彩、强烈广角透视、凌乱道具
